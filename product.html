What does our product do?

These innovative technologies work together seamlessly to ensure optimal health and comfort for individuals. The heart rate monitor provides real-time data to Blink IoT, allowing healthcare professionals to monitor and analyze heart health remotely. Meanwhile, the DHT sensor accurately measures the temperature of the surroundings and automatically controls the fan to maintain a comfortable environment. This dynamic system not only promotes well-being but also demonstrates how technology can enhance our daily lives.
